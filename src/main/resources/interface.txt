Hangman game
Hello, this is a Hangman game,
to start playing, enter 1 on the keyboard!
[1] - Start
[2] - Help
[3] - Exit

Okay, let's choose the difficulty level
[1] - Easy
[2] - Medium
[3] - Hard
[4] - Skip and choose random

And choose a category
[1] - Animals
[2] - Cities
[3] - Fruits and Vegetables
[4] - Skip and choose random


The word is hidden, now enter one letter at a time to guess
__________
If you guess, you are closer to victory,
if you try incorrectly, elements of the gallows will be added
